1. Introduction

Welcome to Student Hub. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle your academic and personal information.

2. Data We Collect

To provide a personalized experience, we collect the following:

  • Account Info: Name, university email, and student ID.
  • Academic Data: Course enrollments and schedule preferences.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our study tools.

3. How We Use Data

We use your data to synchronize your calendar, provide course recommendations, and facilitate peer-to-peer collaboration.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to export your data or delete your account at any time through the "Settings" panel.
