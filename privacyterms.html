Privacy Details

How we safeguard your academic journey at Student Hub.

Data Collection Security Layers Student Rights

Collected Information

We only store information essential for your academic success, such as course registrations and university identifiers.

Encryption Standards

All data is encrypted using AES-256. We utilize end-to-end green-certified servers to host your information.

1. Geo-Location Data

We only access precise location data when you use the "Campus Navigator." This data is never stored on our servers for more than 24 hours.

2. AI & Machine Learning

Our study assistants analyze your input to provide personalized summaries. Your prompts are anonymized before being processed by LLM providers.

3. Cookie Architecture

We use essential cookies for session management. Analytics cookies are opt-in only and help us identify which hub features students use most.

4. Retention Schedule

Active account data is kept indefinitely. Inactive accounts (no login for 2 years) are flagged for deletion, and data is wiped after a 30-day grace period.

5. Biometric Authentication

FaceID or Fingerprint data stays on your device's secure enclave. Student Hub only receives a "success" or "fail" token from your OS.

6. Third-Party Integration

We sync with Google Calendar and Outlook. We only request "Write" permissions to add your class schedule, never "Read" permissions for your private emails.

7. No-Ad Policy

Student Hub is 100% ad-free. We do not share your profile with data brokers or marketing agencies for targeted student advertising.

8. Emergency Disclosure

In extreme cases involving campus safety or legal subpoenas, we may be required to share limited data with university security or law enforcement.

9. Age Requirements

Our platform is designed for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

10. Data Portability

You can request a full JSON export of your academic data at any time via the Settings menu. Your data, your rules.
